LiveEngage - CoBrowse and protected fields

This page shows how to protect form fields and/or buttons in a CoBrowse session from being manipulated by the agent to comply with regulations.
The protection only applies to form elements. To protect an element, add data-protected='true' as an element attribute.
An alternative is to provide a list of classes and/or ID's, which can be added to the back-end to identify protected elements.
